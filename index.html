Join The Community

Membership Information

How do I join?

It's simple: Just show up! Join us at our next event. If you attend, you're part of the local community.

Do I need a certification?

Not at all. Whether you're a seasoned CISSP, a student, or just transitioning into cybersecurity, everyone is welcome!

Are there any fees?

No! There are absolutely zero chapter dues. We want to continue to be 100% free for the community.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do we earn CPEs for attending chapter meetings?
Yes! Just make sure to sign in when you arrive at an event. We can provide you with a certificate of attendance that will work for submitting CPEs to other certifying bodies.
How can my company become a Corporate Sponsor?
Thank you for your support and generosity. Your support would go a long way in helping us achieve our goals and continue to foster a strong and secure cyber environment. We accept cash sponsorship, where in return, you will receive logo placement on our website, along with exposure to all members at our regularly scheduled events. Contact info@isc2chapter-cleveland.us to get involved.
Are you accepting donations?
Yes! We're a 501(c)(3) organization that will gladly take your money. We appreciate any financial contributions to help keep our events completely free for the local cybersecurity community.
Who should I speak with about volunteering, talking, or presenting?
We are always looking for help and new voices! Please refer to the Leadership section on this website for the appropriate officer to contact, or refer to our Membership page to learn more about ways to join in!
